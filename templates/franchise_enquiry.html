vision

Oncore aims to be the main supplier of insurance claim maintenance and repair services in New Zealand. Through our partnerships, and by providing an unmatched quality of customer service, you can begin your business with a high likelihood of an instant workflow and income.

Services

Oncore offers both residential and commercial repairs, maintenance and installations. Examples of our work include leaky home repairs, moss and mould removal, general cleaning, air conditioning installation and carpeting. Oncore provides solutions for whatever maintenance issues a homeowner may be facing.

Point of difference

Oncore is a standout in its field due to its partnerships and processes. Our partnerships with insurance companies and property managers mean that their customers can enjoy a high quality service, and you can start out with a secure business platform.
