We can help you meet the Healthy Homes Standards

From 2021 onwards, all landlords will be required by law to meet certain rental property standards.

Insulation

If your insulation isn’t up to standard, we can top it up or replace it as needed. It’s that simple.

Heating

Oncore will explain your options and, if required, provide your property with a high-quality fixed-heating installation.

Ventilation

We’ll advise you on whether or not your extractor fans and windows meet the standards. If not, we’ll get them sorted.

Drainage and guttering

Our team will improve your property’s drainage, guttering, drainpipes and ground moisture barrier; ensuring there are no surface water or drainage issues.

Draught stopping

Oncore specialises in stopping gaps and holes in walls, ceilings, windows, floors and doors. We can also close in all unused chimneys and fireplaces.
