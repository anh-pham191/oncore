WHAT ARE THE HEALTHY HOME STANDARDS AND HOW WE HELP YOU MEET THEM?

INSULATION

To meet the newly introduced HHS, existing ceiling insulation must be at least 120mm thick. Alternatively, ceiling and underfloor insulation must meet the Requirements set out in the 2008 Build Code

Oncore can help you meet these standards. We can provide you with an insulation inspection. If we find your property is currently not up to standard, we'll update your insulation to meet the requirements.

WHO DO THESE STANDARDS IMPACT?

The Healthy Home Standards affect landlords and their tenants. Landlords will be required by law to meet the minimum housing requirements set out in the HHS

WHEN DO PROPERTY FIXES NEED TO BE IN COMPLIANCE BY?

Most private landlords and boarding houses must ensure their properties comply with the HHS by 1st July 2021. Kainga Ora Homes and Communities and registered Community Housing Provider houses must comply by 1st July 2023.
